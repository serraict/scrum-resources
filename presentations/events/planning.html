Meeting Structure

Time One week sprint, 2 hour timebox.
00-05 Declare sprint goal
05-15 Calendar, team composition and capacity
15-65 Define what will be done
65-115 Define how it will be done
115-120 Conclusion and DoD
## Sprint Planning DoD * Goal known? * Team composition and capcity known? * Highest business value backlog items known? * Are there concerns regarding these items? * Are there any other concerns? * Given all this, what is the team's final forecast for this sprint?
